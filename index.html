Smart Device + VR Training

RAPAEL Smart Device measures movements of the fingers, wrist, elbow and shoulder on a real-time basis. Patients can enjoy a variety of VR games on the RAPAEL App like cooking and fishing.

It is important for stroke patients to practice the same motions repeatedly to re-learn how to move. RAPAEL makes it more efficient.

Stimulates Neuroplasticity


A stroke-affected part of the brain cannot be recovered. However, with training, we can make other parts of the brain re-learn the tasks previously performed by the damaged part. The ability to do so is called neuroplasticity, which can be induced through repetitive training.

Sensorimotor training


Sensorimotor training is a very important part of rehabilitation from brain damage. Training that accompanies active sensory stimulation accelerates the restructuring of the brain. RAPAEL Rehab contents help to invigorate the brain by providing visually and auditorily compelling feedbacks.

Learning schedule algorithm


Neuroplasticity and effectiveness of motor skill learning are maximized as a patient suffering a brain damage performs a more challenging task. RAPAEL’s learning algorithm suggests the most optimal tasks for each patient based on their individual characteristics, and encourages the movements to be “bigger” and “more”.

Rehabilitation Self-Training at Home

Do you feel not as motivated to continue training at home? Do you not have appropriate equipment? RAPAEL Home is the answer to your home rehabilitation training.

Simple and easy use

With its intuitive interface, anyone can start self-training effortlessly.

Training schedule based on algorithm

RAPAEL Device suggests appropriate amount of exercise based on the user data on training.

Regular Update on Training Contents
